Laszlo Edgar T.-Barr

L'Artiste
Born in Miami and raised in the Islands of Grand Bahama. I've used the experiences of my cultural upbringing to help me move along with my art. Creatively inspired by the greats Like Jean Michele-Basquiat and Laszlo Maholy-Nagy, has helped me pursue my styles and look at the world through a different perspective, focal point point or lens. The style connected to the work is fun, stunning and engaging. Some photography from headshots and portraits to landscape, animals, still-life's and candid images like no other.
